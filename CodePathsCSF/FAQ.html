FAQs

  • Our electronic payer ID is 36215. However, make sure to follow claim filing directions on the back of the participant’s id card. Many claims must be filed directly to the participant’s network in which case their electronic payer id should be used.
  • All networks require pre-certification of in-patient admissions. Some networks have additional pre-certification requirements. Remember to check the participant’s ID card for requirements and follow specific network rules.
  • Claim filing instructions vary by network and type of service. Follow the instructions on the back of the participant’s ID card.
  • Electronic claims are usually processed within 2-3 days
    of being received by the Fund and up
    to 30 days when further information must be reviewed such as other insurance information. Paper claims may take up to 30 days from submission.
  • You must send a written appeal within 180 days from the original benefit determination.
    You may file an appeal by completing an appeal form or by letter.
  • The appeal should contain:
  • (1) member’s name and address; (2) member’s ID number;
  • (3) claim number if applicable; (4) patient’s name;
  • (5) relationship of patient to member; (6) date of service if applicable; and
  • (7) EXACT reason you are dissatisfied.
  • Send Appeals to:
    TeamCare
    A Central States Health Plan
    P. O. Box 5126
    Des Plaines, IL 60017-5116
  • The Fund requires a Predetermination of Benefits (PDB) for Hormone
  • Replacement Therapy, Oncotype DX, Proton Beam Therapy, Transplants,
  • services that can be cosmetic and services required by the network.
  • We recommend submitting a PDB for custom or specialized equipment and services
  • that are not considered standard medical care.
  • Some PDB’s should be filed directly with the participant’s network
  • Contact us for specific information.
  • The Fund does not cover procedures that are cosmetic, experimental or investigational in nature. A list of Plan exclusions is available in the Summary Plan

  • Yes, the Plan covers pre-existing conditions.
